iPads FAQ

How are iPads used for teaching in the classroom?

Answer

iPads are used with class activities, like computers and Smart Boards. They also allow for working on homework for other classes.

Why would my child need an iPad?

Answer

There is a new program that is underway called MyWay. It is a way to "make sure that each and every one of our students are prepared to thrive today and excel tomorrow in an ever-changing global society".

What will students be able to access on their iPads?

Answer

Students will be able to access applications from the App Store. There will be applications that are essential to learning that they will have to download. They will also be able to access the school's wireless network on the iPad.

What happens if it damaged or lost?

Answer

The parents are responsible if it needs to be repaired or replaced.

Other than iPads, what other devices are under consideration to be given to students?

Answer

Laptops are another form of technology that may be under consideration to be given to students. Other school districts have been trying out different tablets and laptops for assistance while teaching.


Scenarios

If they need to do a reading for a class, how can they do that without a hardcover book?

Answer

There are e-books for the text books. This allows for an easier way of storing books without the heavy weight.

A student is doing a research project, and she needs as many resources as possible to help her with the project. How can she find resources?

Answer

iTunes U is a collection of free learning resources from renowned institutions. This allows easy access to great resources for research projects or just learning in general.
